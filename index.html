How it works

A secure, privacy-first journey from identity to rewards — fully aligned for users, brands, and researchers.

01

KYC with SwissBorg (one-time)

Verify once through SwissBorg to ensure compliance and security. Your documents stay with SwissBorg — OPI never accesses original KYC files.

03

Set your interests

Pick categories (e.g., sports, music, tech). Your interest graph combines with SBT attributes to match you with relevant surveys and ads — without exposing personal data.

05

Earn fair rewards in OPI

Receive OPI for each validated response or completed ad view. Quality checks and frequency caps ensure fair, anti-spam distribution.

02

Mint anonymous SBT on Galactica

Galactica issues a SoulBound Token with encrypted attributes (age band, country, gender, device…). You can later prove attributes with Zero-Knowledge proofs — no raw data revealed.

04

Get matched surveys & ads

Campaigns target your anonymous persona only. You prove eligibility (e.g., 18+, country, device) via ZK. Higher relevance → better experience for you and better data for brands.

06

Redeem OPI: USDC or vouchers

Convert OPI to USDC via the internal pool or redeem brand vouchers in the marketplace — all inside the closed OPI economy, no external exchanges required.

Marketplace

FAQ

Short answers to the most common questions.

Is my identity public on OPI?

No. You verify once (SwissBorg) and get an anonymous Galactica SBT. You only share the attributes needed for each survey (e.g., country, 18+, device).

Can I trade OPI on external exchanges?

No. OPI is an internal utility token. Conversion happens only via the internal OPI/USDC pool or vouchers on the marketplace.

How do users earn?

By completing surveys and watching targeted ads. Rewards are instant and transparent on‑chain.

What do brands get out of OPI?

Certified audiences with verified attributes (via SBT/ZK), higher data quality, and better ad performance.

Do I keep control of my data?

Yes. You decide what to share for each survey. Zero‑Knowledge lets you prove facts without revealing personal information.
